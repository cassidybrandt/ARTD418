Baked Fresh Daily

Sugarjones, inc was founded in 2011 by Kathy Pomerene, avid baker and creative dreamer.
Our first location opened as a cake shop and small retail bakery on december 10th, 2011 on rural Street in Rockford, il. Word got out that our stuff is good! Now we have 2 locations in the Rockford area!
We take great pride in the fact that we are the only cupcake bakery in the area who bakes fresh daily (our freezer is pretty empty!) using only the best ingredients available to us. We also love to use local vendors for specialty items when we can.
One bite and you can tell that we are making the world a better place, one cupcake at a time.
